The dogwifhat is a Meme started by @Issa and @SpoodahRL

This meme is about making dogs with unqiue hats and using them as your profile picture It created many communites, and discord servers with people sharing there dogs with hats

The generator was created by @RockCrafts in order to help people make them easier and faster. Although this creates basic hats only, there are many people such as in, discord.dogwifahat.com, that help make cool 3d custom hats such as the ones in featured dogs tab.

How to make a dogwifahat?

First find an image. Make sure it is a PNG, JPG, or GIF, Although you can uplaod a gif, it currently will not be animated. This may come at a later date though. Then select the blue Settings button. It will show you many options, You can add a mustache to the dog by selecting, mustache. You can make the hat all black and have a little image by selecting "Black Hat". You can also have a wool hat, by selecting "Wool Hat". Although, you may not have "Black Hat" and "Wool Hat" enabled at the same time. You can make the image Upsidedown by selecting "Upside down"

After you select your desired settings, hit "Generate dogwifahat" to create your image. If you need to move the image, do that by changing the adjust y and the adjust x. If it is zoomed in to far, fix that by adjusting the Zoom setting. When you are happy with your dogwifahat, you may either copy it and paste it where you desire, or you can click the "Here!" button.
